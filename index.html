 Codezilla 2018

Codezilla calls on programmers to put their skills to the test by solving multiple rounds of algorithmic puzzles and win cash prizes worth 15k. In addition to being challenging and fun, Codezilla problems can help you develop your coding and programming skills.

So apply and hack through these round to show your coding prowess.

Competition will be held at T block, MIET Meerut as per schedule.

 Rules

  1. All the participants must register themselves on CodeChef.
  2. Only mietians are eligible for round 1.
  3. Non-mietians will be able to participate in round 2.
  4. Keep CodeChef credentials handy: they will be used to participate in the competition.
  5. This is an individual event. Make sure your machines are equipped with necessary libraries and compilers.
  6. Any form of plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  7. All the participants are required to carry their college ID CARDS.
